HL7, ANSI and the FHIR Standard

FHIR Management GroupMaturity Level: N/AStandards Status: Informative

FHIR Release 5 will not be submitted to ANSI for consideration as a Normative standard. None of the new content in R5 is considered Normative, and will be subject for futher review during the review and ballot process for Release 6. Note that content that was normative for Release 4 remains in effect normative, and will be treated accordingly during the R6 process.
