  Display = Episode
  Definition = This type of team focuses on one specific episode of care with a defined time period or self-limiting process (e.g. 10 visits). The episode of care is determined by the context of use.  For example, a maternity care team over 9 months.

  Id = 4

For example, disease management team or patient centered medical home team.

  Summary = Y

  Binding = CareTeamCategory

  Card. = 0..1
  Summary = Y

  Comments = The meaning/purpose of the team is conveyed in CareTeam.category.  This element may also convey semantics of the team (e.g. \"Red trauma team\"), but it\'s primary purpose is to distinguish between identical teams in a human-friendly way.  (\"Team 18735\" isn\'t as friendly . )
