* Dosage.simple has been removed (FHIR-54651, https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-54651)
  Dosage.simple was redundant since a simple dosage can be expressed as a single step.
  Removed dosdet-1 invariant; added invariant that step.safety shall not be populated
  when only one step is present. Updated documentation and examples.

* Fix entered-in-error definitions for MedicationStatement and MedicationRequest
  (FHIR-54903, https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-54903)
  Corrected the misleading entered-in-error status definitions to simply state
  the resource was entered in error and is not valid.

* Clarify MedicationStatement.reason vs .relatedClinicalInformation
  (FHIR-54451, https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-54451)
  Updated .reason definition to clarify it covers what leads to taking (or not
  taking) a medication. Added clarification to .relatedClinicalInformation that
  it is not the actual indication - that belongs in .reason.
