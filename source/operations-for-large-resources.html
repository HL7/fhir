Operations for Large Resources (e.g., Groups/Lists)

Responsible Owner: [%wgt fhir%] Work GroupStandards Status:Informative

HL7 is working on defining three new operations that make it possible to add and remove items from the list or group, and to retrieve a focused subset of the information in the list and group without having to transmit the entire resource.

These operations are still under development, so they are presently found in the API Incubator.
